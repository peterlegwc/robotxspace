Meetup

RobotX Meetup is a networking event focused on robotics and related topics. We meet once a month to discuss innovations in the robotics industry. RobotX Meetups are the premiere place for robotics enthusiasts to network and collaborate. We invite visionaries and representatives from the industry's leading companies for keynotes, fireside chats and panels on robotics, artificial intelligence, 3D printing, wearables, IoT, smart devices, and more. We offer exclusive networking opportunities, and strive to help this generation understand tomorrow's trends.
